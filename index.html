Welcome to Unity

A modern sanctuary for every heartbeat.

Spread across 10 acres in the heart of Gujarat, with over 440,000 sq. ft. of built-up space, Unity Hospital is the tertiary-care flagship of the Unity Hospitals Group — currently 300+ beds, with the capacity to expand to 400.

Our Centres of Excellence span Cardiac Sciences, Neuro Sciences, Orthopaedics (Knee, Hip, Spine), Oncology, Emergency Medicine and Solid-Organ Transplants, alongside 35+ allied disciplines — all under one roof.
